Research CV

I am a final-year MSc student in Language Technologies at Università di Pisa, where I dedicated myself to machine learning applications to NLP, with a special attention to Transformers-based models.

My research interests lie in the fields of Computational Linguistics and Explainability of LLMs. I maintain a list of my publications and research projects under the Research tab.

I have spent 3 great years studying (mainly) linguistics in Humanities faculty at University of Pavia, from where I received my bachelor’s degree (BA) in 2021, advised by prof. Ilaria Fiorentini.
